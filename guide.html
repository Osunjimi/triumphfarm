Triumph Farms — Catfish Fingerling Care Guide

Pond preparation

Clean, disinfect, liming, fill, stabilize water for 24-48 hours.

Acclimatization

Float bags 10-20 minutes, mix pond water gradually, release gently.

Feeding

Weeks 0-4: 45% protein 3-4x daily. Weeks 4-8: 35-40% protein 2-3x daily.

Water quality

pH 6.5-7.5, temp 26-29°C, keep ammonia low.

Health & disease

Signs: gasping, red patches. Actions: aeration, partial water change, isolate sick fish.
